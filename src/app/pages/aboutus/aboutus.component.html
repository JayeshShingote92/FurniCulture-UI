About Us

Our mission is to provide a gateway to luxury furniture and modern designs to the global audience. To bring you home to comfort, you need decor with outstanding designs and pieces of luxury, you can see and feel. Every detail matters – designs, finishing, stunning appearance, craftsmen touches and colors, and the highest quality materials. It is a guiding force on which innovation, aspirations and ongoing technology thrives. A strong customer focus approach and constant quest for top class quality and service have enabled us to attain social sustainability.

Our team offer a range of modern and multifunctional furniture for your offices and homes, which are the exquisite blend of international designs, quality and finishing. We also develop customization in a way which maximizes the utilization of space and multi-functionality.

Meet Vikram Jangid (Founder & CEO): After 19 years of engrossing experience on Techno-commercial-function from various corporate houses, he returned to his old love: Woodworking. He has a great respect for nature and he always wanted to intertwine it with his overwhelming passion for creativity. At Furniculture, he believes in the revelatory power of illustrations and designs and its ability to elevate simple experiences. Creativity makes us happy: so, let’s make something magnificent and beautiful together.
